biography

Josefine Jyllnor (b. 2001) is an artist from Stockholm, Sweden. Currently based in Stockholm.

Her practice is mainly centered around hand-drawn stop-motion animations presented as infinite loops, often exploring the cyclical nature of human behavior and cognition.

BA in Visual Art & Painting, Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti , Milan.

exhibitions

2025. “Cultural Glitch”. Collective exhibition at Gola Gallery, Milan.

2024. “Diversi Presenti”. Collective exhibition at Società Umanitaria, Milan.

2023. “Dalisi Napoli Oggi". Collective project directed by Vincenzo Castella in connection to the exhibition “Riccardo Dalisi. Radicalmente” at MAXXI Museo Nazionale delle arti del XXI Secolo, Rome.
